TRAMMO GENERAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS (FOB PURCHASE)

1. PRODUCT AND SPECIFICATIONS

Product: Anhydrous Ammonia (NH3)
Specifications: Minimum purity 99.5%, water content maximum 0.5%,
oil content maximum 5 ppm.

2. QUANTITY

Quarterly Quantity: 8,000 metric tons per quarter +/- 10% more or less
at buyer's option. Annual total: 32,000 metric tons. Individual barge
lots of approximately 1,500 metric tons each. Minimum of four (4) barge
lots per quarter.

3. INCOTERMS

INCOTERMS 2020: FOB Seller's terminal, Donaldsonville, Louisiana.

4. PRICE

Price: US $335.00 per metric ton FOB Donaldsonville. Price subject to
quarterly adjustment based on NOLA barge price index published by
Fertecon. Price floor of $300.00 per metric ton.

5. SHIPMENTS AND NOMINATIONS

Shipments / nominations: Buyer shall nominate individual barge lots with
7 days prior notice. Monthly nominations to be spread evenly across the
quarter. Buyer shall provide quarterly nomination 15 days before start
of each quarter.

6. PAYMENT

Payment: By telegraphic transfer within 15 days from bill of lading date.
Late payment interest at 1.5% per annum above SOFR.

7. INSPECTION AND QUANTITY DETERMINATION

Inspection: Quantity determined by certified shore tank gauging at
Seller's terminal. Seller's terminal records shall be final and binding
for invoicing purposes. Bill of lading quantity governs settlement.

8. LAYTIME AND DEMURRAGE

Laytime: Barge laytime shall be 24 running hours from time of NOR.
Demurrage: $6,500 per day or pro rata for barge demurrage. Demurrage
claims must be submitted within 60 calendar days from completion of
loading.

9. FORCE MAJEURE

Force Majeure: Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by Force
Majeure events including but not limited to: floods, hurricanes, lock
closures, river stage below 9 feet at Cairo gauge, government orders,
labor disputes, or equipment failure beyond reasonable control. Notice
within 3 days. River stage below 9 feet at Cairo gauge shall entitle
either party to suspend barge deliveries.

10. DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

Event of Default: Material default not remedied within 48 hours of notice
entitles termination. Interest at 1.5% per annum on late payments. Mutual
right of setoff and netting is preserved.

Penalty for volume shortfall: If Buyer fails to lift the minimum quarterly
quantity (net of tolerance), Buyer shall pay Seller $10.00 per metric ton
on the shortfall volume.

11. DISCLAIMER OF WARRANTIES

Disclaimer of Warranties: SELLER MAKES NO WARRANTY OF ANY KIND, EXPRESS
OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY.

12. CLAIMS

Claims: Written notice of claim within 30 days of delivery. Claims shall
be absolutely barred unless action is commenced within one year from date
of delivery.

13. GOVERNING LAW AND ARBITRATION

Governing Law: Laws of the State of New York shall govern this contract.
Arbitration: Disputes shall be settled by arbitration under SMA rules
in New York.

14. NOTICES

Notices: Notices deemed to be given when sent by courier, fax, or e-mail.

15. MISCELLANEOUS

Miscellaneous: Entire Agreement of the parties. Modifications in writing
only. No Waiver of breach implies waiver of future breach. Assignment
requires consent. Severability applies.
